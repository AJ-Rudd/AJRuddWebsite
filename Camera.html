The Camera Sees All

In this project a polaroid instax camera was wired to a tf-mini lidar sensor using an Arduino. Powered by a Lithium Ion battery the camera was placed around University of Marylands campus. The camera would take photos of people passing when it detected their motion. and print it out for them if they wanted to take it. After being that one person took their photo the rest of the images were put on a wall for a week long art performance. It started with putting the images on the wall in a public space and creating connections using string and tacks. From there connections were inferred that seemingly had no connection to each other to inspire passerby's to ask questions about what was happening. Several conversations occured throughout the week with people interested in the project. One person continued to come back every day and engage with the new content being added daily. The different colors represent a new day.
