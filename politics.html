Political Distraction(prototype)

The goal of this project was to recreate the confusion surrounding politics and the media. I took Instagram comments from two political figures, who were on opposite ends of the political spectrum, and loaded the comments into a text to speech program on Iphones. I utilized bluetooth speakers to magnify the sound, so that it was like the two phones were arguing. The project is intended to be viewed in a dark room with only the sound being heard, and the screens being able to be viewed. Underneath the phones projecting the comments two Arduino screens were placed which held more obscure media information. Under one phone was the number of citizens in the middle east who had been killed since the war in Iraq began, and the other was the release date of Chelsea Manning from prison. Moving forward with this piece the comments, and obscure news stories would be displayed using projectors onto the walls, and the sound would be much more immersive.
